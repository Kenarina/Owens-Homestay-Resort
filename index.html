Relaxation and tranquility in the countryside

About us

Owen's Homestay Resort provides a comfortable, down-to-earth relaxing home feel for a refreshing holiday experience. Located on a 20-acre mixed farm, about 50 Km North of Kampala Capital City, Owen's Homestay Resort offers tranquility away from the hustle and bustle of city. Below are some the activities that might interest you during your stay, of course depending of the season of the year.

Activities

Whether you want to relax and enjoy the sun or discover what the farm has to offer, there are several stress-free activities to keep you engaged with nature in relaxed environment. These include:

  • Bird watching
  • Fruit picking
  • Honey harvesting
  • Sport fishing
  • Kitchen Gardening
